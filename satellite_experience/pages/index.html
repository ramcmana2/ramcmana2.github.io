Welcome to the Psyche Satellite Mission AR Experience

In this experience, users will learn more about the Psyche satellite. Users will be able to explore the current mission progress and timeline, the spacecraft and its equipment, and details about mission itself. Clicking the following launch button will start an interactive AR experience for the user where they can click on different elements of the satellite to learn facts about the mission, distance traveled, the satellite equipment, and resources used.
